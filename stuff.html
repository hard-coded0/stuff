• X Reflection: (x,y)->(x,-y)
  • Y Reflection: (x,y)->(-x,y)
  • Y=X Reflection: (x,y)->(y,x)
  • Y=-X Reflection: (x,y)->(-y,-x)
  • Y=b Reflection: (x,y)->(x,2b-y)
  • X=b Reflection: (x,y)->(2b-x,y)
  • 90 clock: (x,y)->(y,-x)
  • 90 counterclock: (x,y)->(-y,x)
  • 180: (x,y)->(-x,-y)
  • 90 clock ab: (x,y)->(a+(y-b),b+(a-x))
  • 90 counterclock ab: (x,y)->(a+(b-y),b+(x-a))
  • 180 ab: (x,y)->(a+(a-x),b+(b-y))
